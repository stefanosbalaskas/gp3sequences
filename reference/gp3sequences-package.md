# gp3sequences: Transparent Analysis of Ordered Categorical Sequences

`gp3sequences` provides transparent, reproducible, and auditable tools
for working with ordered categorical sequences represented in ordinary
long-format data frames.

## Details

The package is designed around explicit column mappings, deterministic
ordering, documented preprocessing policies, machine-readable
diagnostics, and conservative structural summaries. It is not restricted
to eye-tracking data or to any particular device or export format.

## Interpretation boundary

Sequence outputs describe behavioural or structural patterns. They do
not independently establish emotion, cognition, comprehension,
personality, intention, diagnosis, deception, or other psychological
attributes. Substantive interpretation requires an appropriate study
design and external evidence.
